Privacy Policy

Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application and web site Arts & Rec Guide developed by Dan Morgan Designs (“the App(s)”). It explains how information is handled when you use any of these apps.

Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no user accounts, logins, analytics, or tracking of any kind. The App operates entirely on your device without connecting to external services that collect information about you.

Data Storage and Transmission

No personal or device data is transmitted to external servers. The only network requests made by the App are to retrieve static image files hosted in a private cloud storage bucket controlled by the developer. These images are owned and managed solely by Dan Morgan Designs.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with or depend on any third-party analytics, advertising, or social media platforms. It does not use cookies or local storage to track user behavior.

Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

Any future updates to this policy will be posted on this page. Material changes will be noted with an updated “Last updated” date above.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please use this contact form.
