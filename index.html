Overland Park Arts & Rec Guide

This site helps you explore the arboretum, farmstead, and public arts installations supported by the Arts and Recreation Foundation of Overland Park.

You can hide or show different points of interest (POIs) categories and hiking trails by selecting the layers icon on the map view.

Selecting a POI icon on the map view will display helpful information about it. Your location on the map can be shown by selecting the location icon.

For optimal user experience when cell services is spotty, please download the app from the App Store.
