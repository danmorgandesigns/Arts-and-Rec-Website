Overland Park Arts & Rec Guide

The Overland Park Arts & Rec Guide is a mobile-friendly website and iPhone/iPad app that that helps you explore the Overland Park Aboretum & Botanical Gardens, Deanna Rose Children's Farmstead, and public arts installations supported by the Arts and Recreation Foundation of Overland Park.

You can hide or show different points of interest (POIs) categories and hiking trails by selecting the layers icon on the map view.

Selecting one of the point of interest icons ( ) on the map view will display helpful information about it. Your location on the map can be shown by selecting the location icon .

For optimal user experience when cell services is spotty, please download the app from the Apple App Store.
